About

I specialize in motion design at the intersection of branding and digital products. Previously, I worked in roles including UI/UX designer, which gave me a strong understanding of product design, implementation processes, and collaboration with development teams. I create animations that help users better understand products, enhance the overall user experience, and give brands a distinctive character. I am particularly interested in transforming static brand systems into motion that becomes a natural part of the product and strengthens its perception.
